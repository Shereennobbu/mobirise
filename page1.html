To Our Investors  

Who are we?

Jiffy is all about creating convenience.

We’re a personal service delivery company that saves you Time, by doing the little things for you.

What do we mean?

We deliver anything from fast foods, alcohol, cigarettes and condoms, to your best friends Sunday-kos skaftin. We’re focused on creating a community of convenience by placing importance on executing each unique customer order with efficiency and flair. And to top it all off, a unique customer experience for all users. You can’t buy time, we’re just the closest thing to it.

 The opportunity?

People want the freedom to shop from anywhere they want and with no restrictions on what time of day it is. Jiffy is the only on-demand delivery service that serves this exact need.

There are approximately 12000 students that live in Auckland park and Melville, and 4000 employees that work in office blocks within the area. Students need more than just food, and employees only have a 1 hour lunch break every day to do their errands but never have enough time to do so. No one is currently serving these two classes of people’s needs adequately.

Highlights

Jiffy is young, hungry and rapidly growing. Since our official launch on 1st of November 2017 to March 2018 , Jiffy has achieved the following growth: 400+ Unique customers, 700+orders completed and over R31 000 in Total sales, with a 55% Gross profit margin.


 Our Value proposition

It is indeed true that time wasted never returns and Jiffy advocates for this old saying. The use of time teaches one about the value of life. When one thinks about all the menial activities that consume their time, they can only regret how much value they have missed out on. Jiffy exists to add value in all respects by completing those menial activities/errands so that the customer can live a more meaningful life. In addition to that, the customer gets their intended goods and services in the comfort of their space and at a time that suits them, and at a reasonable price.

Moreover we are a start-up company which employs young, vibrant and ambitious individuals. We believe in actively engaging with our customers in order to truly understand their needs and by so doing, we've created a service which is tailor made for them.

We are a growth driven company, with that being said our goal is to achieve R150 million market capitalization by 2023.
